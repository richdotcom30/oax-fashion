The Atelier

Where Craft Meets Soul

In our private atelier, time moves differently. Every garment is a dialogue between the designer's vision and the artisan's hand. We reject mass production in favor of slow, deliberate creation.

From the selection of raw silk to the final hand-stitched hem, our process is an uncompromising pursuit of perfection.

Our Journey

2018

The Inception

Born from a desire to redefine evening wear, OAX Fashion was founded in a small studio with a single collection of five red dresses.

2019

First Atelier

We opened our first physical atelier, allowing clients to experience the tactile quality of our fabrics firsthand.

2021

Global Launch

OAX expanded internationally, bringing our unique blend of modern luxury to fashion capitals around the world.

2024

The Future

Innovating with sustainable materials and digital craftsmanship, we continue to define the future of luxury fashion.
